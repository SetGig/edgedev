EDGEDev

EdgeDev Construction and Trading Inc. was founded with the aim of providing exceptional and value-for-money products, and construction project management and general contracting services.
EdgeDev is comprised of key people who have extensive and combined decades of experience in their respective fields with high-level skills, unparalleled dedication and commitment to providing excellent service and honest partnership.

OUR MISSION

EdgeDev was established with the aim of distinguishing our company as the builder and supplier of choice to those seeking dedicated, high quality, and value for money products and services. We are not gratified with just being one of the many but we strive to be consistently significant by adapting to current market needs and challenges.

OUR VISION

EdgeDev envisions itself as the company that raises the level of professionalism, integrity, ethical practice, and performance within the construction industry so as to provide foundation for long-term customer relations and growth; committed to the genuine satisfaction and success of our business partners, clients and employees.
